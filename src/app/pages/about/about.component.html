About the Awards

Honoring excellence and celebrating the vibrant culture of Zimbabwe's most dynamic music genre

Our Mission

To celebrate and promote the cultural significance of Zimdancehall music, recognizing artistic excellence while fostering growth and innovation within the genre. We aim to create a platform that honors the voices shaping Zimbabwe's musical landscape.

Our Journey

Established in 2018, the Zimdancehall Music Awards have become the premier platform for recognizing talent in Zimbabwe's fastest-growing music movement. What began as a small community initiative has grown into a nationally celebrated event that honors our unique musical identity and cultural heritage.
