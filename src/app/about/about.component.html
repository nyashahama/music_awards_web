About the Awards

Honoring excellence in Zimbabwe's most vibrant music genre

Our Mission

To celebrate and promote the cultural significance of Zimdancehall music, recognizing artistic excellence while fostering growth and innovation within the genre.

History

Established in 2018, the Zimdancehall Music Awards have become the premier platform for recognizing talent in Zimbabwe's fastest-growing music movement. What began as a small community initiative has grown into a nationally televised event celebrating our unique musical identity.
